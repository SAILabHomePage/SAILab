Hi there, and welcome! We're SAILab, a research group at Soongsil University exploring how AI works from the ground up — and how to make it general, scalable, and efficient enough to thrive in an academic lab. If you're a student who's curious about doing real AI research, you're exactly who this page is for. Take a look around, and feel free to say hello.
